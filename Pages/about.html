FAQ

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and Sundays from 12:00 PM to 10:00 PM. We recommend making a reservation for dinner service, especially on weekends.

Do you offer vegetarian and vegan options?

Yes! We have an extensive selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes on our menu. Our chefs are also happy to accommodate special dietary requirements with advance notice.

Where do you source your produce?

We work exclusively with local farmers and sustainable suppliers to ensure the freshest, highest quality ingredients. All our produce is organic and sourced within 100 miles of our restaurant.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide full-service catering for events of all sizes, from intimate gatherings to large corporate events. Contact us directly to discuss your catering needs and receive a custom quote.

How can I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation by calling us at +1 (555) 123-4567, booking online through our website, or using popular reservation platforms. We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance for optimal availability.
